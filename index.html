Why would I use Playtime?

Every dog owner knows that the best way to tire their dog out is to let it play with other dogs. A tired dog is a happy dog. Use Playtime to find new doggy parks and doggy friends. Let other dogs know when yours is out to play, and they'll get notified to join the fun!

See features in action:

Join Parks

Find dog parks near you and join them to see which dogs go there

Doggy Friends

Follow dogs that play well with your dog and see what they're up to

Schedule Playtimes

Schedule playtimes in advance to allow other dogs to join in the fun

Let's Hang Out

Get notified when your dog's friends are at the park
